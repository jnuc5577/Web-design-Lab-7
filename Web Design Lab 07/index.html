Bio:

Stephen Sanchez is an American singer. He become famous from his song "Until I Found You" with nearly a billion streams on Spotify. He was inspired by figures like Frank Sinatra and The Platters. he released his debut EP, "What Was, Not Now" in 2021. He released his latest album, "Angel Face" in 2023. "Angel Face" is a story in the form of an album about a love story set 59 years ago in a club called "The Angel".
